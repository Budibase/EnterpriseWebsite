Knights Brown, a construction and engineering company, adopted Budibase to accelerate the development of internal tools. The platform's capabilities allowed them to build applications quickly, reducing the time and cost associated with traditional development methods.

By leveraging Budibase's low-code approach, Knights Brown was able to modernize their project management and site operations, creating custom solutions that fit their specific construction workflow requirements.

The platform enabled rapid digitization of site inspections, safety checks, and progress reporting, bringing real-time visibility to projects across multiple construction sites.
