As a full-range supplier of mechanized tunnelling technology, Herrenknecht supports the construction industry in all underground challenges. With experience from over 5,300 projects, Herrenknecht has established itself as the global leader in tunnel boring machine manufacturing.

Herrenknecht faced challenges in developing internal tools efficiently. By adopting Budibase, they were able to build custom tools faster, improving their internal processes and productivity. The platform's ease of use and flexibility allowed them to address specific needs without extensive development resources.

The user-friendly interface of Budibase enabled non-technical staff to participate in application development, accelerating digital transformation across the organization.
