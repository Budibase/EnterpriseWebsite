Ströer-x, a digital marketing company and part of Germany's largest out-of-home media company, leveraged Budibase to create custom applications that improved their operational workflows.

The platform's flexibility and ease of use enabled them to develop solutions that met their specific needs without extensive coding. This allowed the team to rapidly build and deploy tools for campaign management, inventory control, and performance analytics.

By adopting Budibase, Ströer-x was able to streamline their digital advertising operations and create self-service portals for advertisers, enhancing both internal efficiency and client experience.
